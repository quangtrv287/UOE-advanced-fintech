6 Jars Financial Management System

Welcome to the 6 Jars Financial Management System game! In this game, you'll learn about effective financial management by allocating your income into different categories represented by jars. Each jar serves a specific purpose:

  • Necessities: This jar is for essential expenses such as rent, groceries, utilities, and transportation.
  • Financial Freedom Account: Allocate money to this jar to build your emergency fund and cover unexpected expenses.
  • Education: Invest in your future by setting aside funds for educational purposes such as courses, books, or skill development.
  • Long-term Savings for Spending: Save for long-term goals like a vacation, purchasing a car, or home renovations.
  • Play: Allow yourself some discretionary spending for entertainment, hobbies, or leisure activities.
  • Give: Share your wealth with others by donating to charities or supporting causes you care about.

Your objective is to allocate your monthly income wisely among these jars while adhering to the following rules:

  • Start with 100 points.
  • Input your income and expenses.
  • Based on your spending and income, we will calculate your point which indicates how well your manage your money
